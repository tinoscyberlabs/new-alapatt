About 60 years ago, late Mr P T Antony set foot into retail jewellery business by starting ‘Alapatt Jewellery’, making him one among the pioneers in the industry in Kerala. He was considered a visionary and is highly honoured as a business legend in Kerala.

In the late 90’s, Mr. Jose Alapatt, son of Mr P T Antony, took over the vision of his father and has become successful in taking the retail jewellery business into newer heights. He established two major jewellery retail outlets and was instrumental in making ‘Alapatt’ a household brand name. Under the valuable guidance of Mr Jose Alapatt, the House of Alapatt forays into e-commerce through the new ‘Alapatt Diamonds website’ offering you an ultimate retail jewellery shopping experience.
